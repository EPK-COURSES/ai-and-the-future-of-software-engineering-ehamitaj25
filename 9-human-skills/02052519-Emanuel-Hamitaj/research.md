# Human Skills That Remain Important in the AI Era

## Introduction

Artificial intelligence is changing software engineering by automating repetitive coding tasks, supporting debugging, generating documentation, and helping developers work faster. However, software engineering is not only about writing code. It also requires communication, teamwork, creativity, ethical reasoning, leadership, decision-making, problem solving, client interaction, and systems thinking.

This research will examine which human-centered software engineering skills remain difficult for AI to replace and why these skills will continue to be important in the future.
